About me

Hey. My name is Jamilya, but people call me Jema. I am a creative professional passionate about fashion, design, and innovations. Over the past 6 years, I have worked with many brands such as Lenovo, Wolt, Realme, OLX, AirVision, Absolut, Beefeater, and others.

Since childhood, I have often traveled and lived in different countries and continents of the world, which gave me an even more unique vision of the world, but during quarantine I returned for 2 years to Kazakhstan, the city of Almaty, the city where I was born and where I created projects that I'm proud.

Now I have moved to Europe and I want to further develop my potential and career in the creative field.

I enjoy creating integrated campaigns, creative concepts, and creative solutions with technology, data, and big ideas.
